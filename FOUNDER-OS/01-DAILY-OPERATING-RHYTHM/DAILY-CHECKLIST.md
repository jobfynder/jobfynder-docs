# Daily Checklist

The Daily Checklist is the founder's execution checklist for every working day.

Its purpose is to reduce decision fatigue, maintain focus, and ensure that every day produces a meaningful, completed outcome aligned with the current sprint.

---

## Daily Startup Checklist

### Mission Alignment

- [ ] Does today's work support the Jobfynder mission?
- [ ] Is today's work aligned with the current sprint?
- [ ] Does today's outcome create long-term value?

---

### Focus

- [ ] Have I defined today's single outcome?
- [ ] Is there only one primary artifact?
- [ ] Am I within the WIP limit (Maximum 3 active tasks)?
- [ ] Have all new ideas been added to the Parking Lot?

---

### Execution

- [ ] Has today's artifact entered the correct stage?
    - [ ] Discover
    - [ ] Design
    - [ ] Review
    - [ ] Specification Frozen
    - [ ] Implementation
    - [ ] Verification
    - [ ] Commit
    - [ ] Retrospective

---

### Decision Quality

Before starting work, ask:

- [ ] Is this the highest priority today?
- [ ] Does this compound future value?
- [ ] Does this reduce future effort?
- [ ] Would Future Me thank Present Me for completing this?

---

### Attention Protection

- [ ] Have I avoided unnecessary context switching?
- [ ] Am I working on today's sprint instead of new ideas?
- [ ] Have I intentionally said "Not Now" to lower-priority work?

---

### End-of-Day Review

- [ ] Was today's outcome completed?
- [ ] Was the artifact committed to GitHub?
- [ ] Were new lessons captured?
- [ ] Were improvements added to the Evolution Queue?
- [ ] Is tomorrow's first task already identified?

---

## Daily Reflection

Answer these questions before ending the day:

1. What did I complete today?
2. What created the most value?
3. What distracted me?
4. What did I learn?
5. What should become a reusable system?
6. What should be automated?
7. What is tomorrow's first artifact?

---

## Guiding Principle

> A successful day is measured by the completion of meaningful artifacts—not by the amount of activity performed.
